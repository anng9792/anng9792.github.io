Throw the Ball To Catch the Numbers!

Congrats! You entered your number!

Only 10 digits allowed.

Instructions

  1. Click and drag the PokeBall at the bottom of the screen upwards towards the number you want to catch.
  2. Release your left mouse button to send the ball flying towards the number.
  3. The speed of your throw affects how far your ball travels.
  4. There's a chance your ball will "Miss!" but you can keep trying!
  5. Click "Submit" when you've entered your number.
  6. Click "Reset Number" to restart and try again.
